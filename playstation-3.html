PlayStation 3 (2006)

The PlayStation 3, released in 2006, marked a significant leap in gaming technology with its adoption of Blu-ray Disc as the primary media format, which provided higher storage capacity and enhanced visual fidelity. Its introduction of features like the PlayStation Network and support for high-definition graphics broadened the scope of online multiplayer gaming and digital content delivery. Despite a challenging launch, the PS3's innovative technology and robust library ultimately established it as a pivotal platform in the evolution of gaming.
