PlayStation 2 (2000)

The PlayStation 2, launched in 2000, was groundbreaking for its inclusion of a built-in DVD player, which not only expanded its functionality but also contributed significantly to the widespread adoption of DVDs. Its vast library of games, including critically acclaimed titles and innovative franchises, solidified its place as one of the best-selling consoles of all time and set a new standard for gaming experiences. The PlayStation 2's success cemented Sony's dominance in the gaming industry and paved the way for future advancements in gaming technology and media integration.
